Known Limitations & Uncertainties

  • No Experimental Validation: Direct observation of quantum decoherence near black holes is currently impossible.
  • Simplified Model: Full quantum gravity treatment would require theories beyond current physics (string theory, loop quantum gravity).
  • Parameter Uncertainty: Phenomenological constants have uncertainty ranges of ±50% or more.
  • Classical Approximations: Uses semiclassical gravity; full quantum effects may differ significantly.
  • No Backreaction: Ignores how quantum decoherence might affect spacetime geometry itself.
